# **App Name**: HalalConnect

## Core Features:

- Halal Certification Request Form: Allow UMKM's to submit a request for Halal certification by filling out a comprehensive form with required details such as NIB, PIRT, and material composition.
- Document Upload and Management: Enable users to upload necessary documents for Halal certification, such as NIB, PIRT, and material compositions, with progress tracking and status updates.
- Real-time Progress Tracking: Offer buyers/UMKM a dashboard to monitor the progress of their Halal certification, from consultation and audit to testing and certificate issuance.
- Vendor Dashboard for Halal Certification: Provide vendors with a dashboard to view new certification requests, manage progress, and communicate with buyers.
- Fixed-Price Commodity Contracts: Enable vendors to list staple products (rice, chili, oil, etc.) with fixed 12-month contract prices, allowing UMKM's to secure stable pricing.
- Vendor Stock and Contract Management: Allow vendors to input and manage their stock levels, contract prices, and per-buyer contract limits for staple products.
- Smart Recommendation tool for Vendor Selection: Based on user requirements for halal certification or staple goods contracts, use AI to provide an optimal short list of vendors, taking into consideration parameters that are most important to the user.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Halal Green (#1C8A4B) to evoke trust and reliability, reflecting Halal compliance.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0) for a clean, minimalist feel.
- Accent color: Gold (#D0A300) to highlight premium contract options, providing a touch of sophistication and signaling higher-value offerings.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', sans-serif, to match a clean and fashionable brand identity.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, to provide a little more warmth to body text and blocks of text.
- Simple and clean icons to represent various functions and categories, ensuring clarity without clutter.
- Use of cards to display information on the main pages, like certification application or fixed price contracts.
- Subtle animations for updating the progress bar to visually engage the user when viewing updates on a request or product delivery.
